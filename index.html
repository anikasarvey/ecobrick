What Are EcoBricks?

What is an EcoBrick?

An EcoBrick is a plastic bottle packed with non-recyclable plastic waste to create a reusable building block. It's a simple solution that transforms waste into valuable construction material.

How to Make One

  • Clean & dry a plastic bottle (0.5L-1.5L)
  • Pack with non-recyclable plastics
  • Use a stick to compress tightly
  • Aim for 0.33g per ml density
  • Cap securely and record weight

What to Include

  • Chip bags & candy wrappers
  • Plastic bags & food packaging
  • Cellophane & bubble wrap
  • Styrofoam (broken small)
  • Non-recyclable plastic films

What to Avoid

  • Glass, metal, or organic waste
  • Recyclable plastics
  • Hazardous materials
  • Sharp or dangerous objects
  • Wet or dirty materials

Environmental Impact

  • Reduces landfill waste
  • Creates durable building materials
  • Raises plastic consumption awareness
  • Builds community engagement
  • Enables sustainable construction

Uses for EcoBricks

  • Garden benches & furniture
  • Wall construction
  • Playground equipment
  • Community structures
  • Art installations
